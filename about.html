Our Goal

The goal of Azia is to make sure that stray animals are safe and well cared for. Animals roam around, cold, hungry, and scared. Our goal is to put an end to this. Animals deserve a warm, caring home to grow old in, with a family ready to provide for them and live the lives they deserve to live. Azia is also prepared to protect animals from mistreatment from their existing owners. Animals are being left outside with barely any food and water in their bowls by their so called owners, making them sick, frail, abused, etc. We want to put a stop to this mistreatment and provide resources for the animals and people who know about animals being mistreated.

Who is behind Azia?

Abe Adjmi, Amanda Loeung, Isabelle Westgate, and Zuri Palmer are the brains behind Azia. Azia was founded in 2024. Azia is located in Pace University in Manhattan, New York.
